History of Wotowoto

Wotowoto is a fine art whereby a group of boys (ten to twenty) team up against one of their own for a short period of time (one to three minutes) to beat the spirit out of him, they'll beat his as if he stole a goat at a local market


Wotowoto has evolved over the past few months, since it has been discovered in early July 2022 , Wotowoto kept evolving rapidly over the months.


Feels like yesterday when Wotowoto was invented. It just feels so nice, unless you're the victim
